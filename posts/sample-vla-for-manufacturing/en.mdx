## What is VLA

Vision-Language-Action (VLA) models are unified models that understand visual information and language commands, converting them into physical robot actions. Recent models like RT-2, Octo, and OpenVLA have garnered significant attention in the robotic manipulation field.

## Manufacturing Potential

Cosmetics manufacturing often involves high-mix, low-volume production where traditional industrial robots lack flexibility. If VLA models could enable task specification through natural language commands alone, manufacturing line flexibility could improve dramatically.
