## RT-2 Overview

RT-2 fine-tunes a large Vision-Language Model (VLM) on robot action data, directly transferring visual-language knowledge learned from the web to robotic control.

### Key Contributions

- Transfers internet-scale visual-language pre-training knowledge to robot actions
- New paradigm of representing actions as language tokens
- Generalization to novel objects and concepts unseen during training

## Limitations

- Inference speed unsuitable for real-time control (below 5Hz)
- Large model size makes edge device deployment difficult
- Fails to meet precision requirements of real manufacturing environments
