## Core Idea

Diffusion Policy applies diffusion models — hugely successful in image generation — to robot action generation. Unlike conventional methods that predict single actions, it starts from noise and gradually generates action sequences.

## Why It Matters

- Naturally represents multi-modal action distributions
- Generates long action sequences (action chunks) in one pass
- Outperforms existing methods on real robot tasks

> "The key insight is generating actions the way we generate images."
