## Key notes (dummy content)

When designing a Physical AI manufacturing system, success depends not only on model quality but also on the full operational loop:

1. **Task definition**: define process steps and success criteria
2. **Data capture**: collect vision/force/state logs
3. **Policy improvement**: improve model- or rule-based control
4. **On-site validation**: verify safety and repeatability
5. **Operations feedback**: accumulate failure patterns and retraining points

## Why the data loop matters (dummy)

In early pilots, a strong **observe-log-improve loop** often matters more than a sophisticated model.  
This is especially true in manufacturing, where structured logs for failure classification and root-cause tracing are critical.
