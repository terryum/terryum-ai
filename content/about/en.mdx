## Starting with Robots

I built my foundation in robotics studying Mechanical & Aerospace Engineering at Seoul National University. After graduation, I developed defense robots at LIG Nex1 and research robots at KIST, gaining hands-on engineering experience.

## Discovering Deep Learning

At the University of Waterloo, I researched applying deep learning to human and robot motion. This experience convinced me that AI could fundamentally transform robotics.

## Founding a Beauty-AI Startup

In 2019, I left my PhD program and returned to Korea to found ART Lab (AI & Robotics Tech Lab). We brought AI and robotics technology to the beauty industry, creating innovative solutions for skin analysis and personalized cosmetics recommendations.

## Cosmax and Physical AI

After being acquired by Cosmax, the world's #1 cosmetics ODM company, in 2024, I took on the role of AI & Robotics Tech Lead. I'm now on a mission to revolutionize manufacturing with Physical AI, integrating my experiences across robotics, AI, beauty startups, and cosmetics manufacturing.
